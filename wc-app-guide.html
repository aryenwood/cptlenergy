Admin & Manager Guide

Set up your team, configure branding, and manage your org from day one

Getting In
1
Open Your Invite Link
You'll receive a text message from Aryen at WC App with a personalized link and a 6-character invite code. Tap the link -- it opens the app in your browser.
What the invite looks like
Hey Olashawan! 👋
You've been set up as the admin for Triton Energy on WC App.

Your setup code:
WC4X9K

https://tritonenergy.netlify.app
2
Enter Your Invite Code
Tap "Yes — Enter Code" on the first screen and type your 6-character code. The app verifies it and connects you to your org automatically.
💡 Your code expires in 30 days and can only be used once. If you have issues, contact your WC App rep.
3
Sign In with Google
Tap "Continue with Google" and sign in with your Google account. This is how you'll log in every time -- no separate password needed.
4
Complete Your Profile
The app walks you through a short setup -- your name, phone number, and region. This populates your rep card and business card automatically.
Name Phone Region Profile Photo
Setting Up Your Team
5
Invite Your Reps
Go to Admin → Sales Team → Invite Rep. Select their role (Canvasser or Closer), generate a code, and share it via text. Each rep gets their own unique code.
Invite flow
Tap "Invite Rep" in Sales Team section
Select role: Canvasser or Closer
Generate code → WC6-character code appears
Tap "Share via Message" → SMS sends automatically
⚠️ Rep invite codes expire in 7 days. Generate fresh codes if a rep hasn't signed up yet.
6
Configure Your Quick Links
In Admin Settings, add your team's quoting tool URL (GoSolo, etc.) and any other tools they use daily. These appear as quick-access buttons on every rep's dashboard.
7
Add Hub Resources
Go to the Hub tab to upload training videos, sales documents, and pitch resources your reps can access in the field. Organized into Videos, Books, and Documents sections.
Managing Your Team
8
Monitor Activity in Real Time
The Manager Hub on your dashboard shows every rep's activity as it happens -- doors knocked, appointments set, and conversions. Filter by rep or view the full team.
9
Review Recordings
When reps use On The Doors mode and save a recording, it's stored under that customer's address record. Go to CRM → tap any address → see the full activity timeline with playable recordings.
🎙️ Recordings are saved to secure cloud storage. Only you and the rep who recorded it can access them.
10
Leaderboard & Performance
The Board tab shows ranked performance across your team -- doors knocked, contacts made, appointments set, and conversion rates. Updated in real time as reps log activity.

Sales Rep Guide

Everything you need to start knocking doors and closing deals today

Getting Started
1
Open Your Invite Link
Your manager will text you a link and a 6-character code. Tap the link -- it opens the WC App in your browser with your company's branding already loaded.
📱 For the best experience, add the app to your home screen after signing in. Tap the share icon in your browser → "Add to Home Screen".
2
Enter Your Code & Sign In
Tap "Yes — Enter Code", type your invite code, then sign in with Google. Your role and team are set automatically -- you don't need to pick anything.
3
Complete Your Profile
Add your phone number, region, and a profile photo. This auto-fills your digital business card so customers can contact you directly.
Your Daily Workflow
4
Start Your Day — Toggle "On The Doors"
When you're heading out, toggle On The Doors on your home screen. This activates focus mode and lets you record conversations at each door.
OTD Recording Flow
Walk up to the door
Tap the mic button to start recording
Have your conversation naturally
Tap mic again to stop
Tap "TAP TO DISPO" → select outcome
Choose Save or Skip on the recording
🎙️ Recording laws vary by state. You are responsible for compliance in your area. When in doubt, let the homeowner know you're recording for training purposes.
5
Log Every Door
Tap Log Door on your home screen or use the quick-log button on the Map tab. Fill in the address, select your outcome, and add notes. GPS captures the location automatically.
No Answer Callback Not Interested Appointment Sold
6
Book an Appointment
Tap Book Appt from the home screen. Fill in the customer details, assign a closer, and submit. The closer gets a push notification instantly and it appears on both calendars.
Required information
📅 Date & Time
👤 Customer Name & Contact
🏠 Address
⚡ Utility Company & Avg Bill
👔 Assigned Closer
7
Track Your Stats
Your dashboard shows today's doors, total contacts, appointments set, and your conversion rate -- updated in real time as you log activity. Your streak and rank update daily.
Tools That Make You Better
8
AI Brain — Practice Your Pitch
Go to the AI tab and select a persona to practice against. The AI plays a skeptical homeowner, a price-focused buyer, or a technical researcher. Get real-time feedback on your pitch.
Available personas
😤 The Skeptic 💰 Budget-Conscious 🔍 The Researcher ✅ Ready to Buy
9
The Map — Know Your Territory
The Map tab shows every door you've knocked, color-coded by outcome. Tap any pin to see the full history. Use satellite view to spot solar panels already installed nearby.
10
The Hub — Learn from the Best
The Hub tab has training videos, pitch guides, and resources your manager has loaded. 30 minutes of study a week compounds fast over a season.
